You are human, and as such, you learn things by *doing them*, not just by reading and reading books and trying to absorb everything from them.
[As lukipuki puts it](https://github.com/lukipuki/contest-wiki/blob/master/get-better.md#learning-algorithms-and-data-structures), reading about mountain bikes doesn't actually teach you how to ride one.

Practice.
Practice. 
Practice.

Don't study too much.
Start solving problems, tackle them and learn on the way.

*Mistakes inform us about our shortcomings better than we do ourselves*.
