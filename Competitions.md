*Competitive programming* is just that, **competition**.

If you wish to succeed in *CP* you need to compete **a lot**.
Being able to work under pressure and stress is a valuable skill and one we often avoid working on due to how uncomfortable it is.

*CP* helps your programming skills immensely, even if in the industry a lot of problems aren't even related to algorithmic thinking, but still, we all benefit from learning math and English in school not because we all will become mathematicians of linguists, but rather because of how it helps our brains process information.

But above all else, make sure you're having fun!
It's no use if you get good at this and not enjoy doing it in the slightest.

Think of the future and the good things this'll bring to your life.
