Intelligence is not something that can be changed willy-nilly, at least not in great amounts.

We are born with a certain level of intelligence and we can *slightly* increase it not by submitting ourselves to countless hours of mindless training, but by being very deliberate on the *quality* of our training and taking time to do other things.

Challenge yourself with tasks that are just a bit beyond your current skill level, live and breathe on the *[zone of proximal development](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zone_of_proximal_development)*.

As your intelligence increases, you will not have to constantly break every problem down into smaller parts since you'd already familiarised yourself with similar tasks and be able to process them quickly.
